Haven
You have a haven, a safe place to work (pick three).
Haven Options
  • Lore Library: When you hit the books, take +1 forward to Investigate A Mystery (as long as historical reference works are appropriate).
  • Mystical Library: If you use your library's occult tomes and grimoires to prepare yourself, take +1 forward to Use Magic.
  • Protection Spells: Your haven is safe from magic and the occult, and practitioners cannot enter. Practitioners might be able to do something special to evade the wards, but not easily.
  • Armory: When you need a special weapon, roll +Occult. 10+: you have it (plenty if that matters). 7–9: you have it, but only the minimum. Miss: you've got the wrong thing.
  • Infirmary: You can heal people and have the space for one or two to recuperate. The Keeper will tell you how long recovery takes and if you need extra supplies or help.
  • Workshop: Space for building and repairing guns, cars, and gadgets. Work with the Keeper to determine time and supplies.
  • Oubliette: An isolated room from every kind of practitioner, spirit, or magic you know about. Anything stashed there can't be found, can't do any magic, and can't get out.
  • Panic Room: Essential supplies; protected by normal and mystical means. You can hide there for a few days, safe from pretty much anything.
  • Magical Laboratory: A mystical lab with weird ingredients and tools useful for casting spells (Use Magic, big magic, and similar moves).
